About The Seasonal Executive Pass

Jurassic Park's Seasonal Executive Pass offers everything the executive pass offers for a whole season! As well as getting acess to the park for a whole season, you will be given free entry to all park services, skip lines, and be treated like an executive! You will be able to meet key people who were able to make this park possible, such as the likes of John Hammond, Dr. Henry Wu, Ian Malcolm, and more!

There is a $300 insurance and liability fee for any injuries that may occur when interacting with prehistoric wildlife. Extra taxes apply for all tier levels.
