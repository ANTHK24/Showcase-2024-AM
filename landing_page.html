Basic Passes

Exploration Pass - $1,000

The exploration pass lets you enter the basic park zones with a guided tour.

Expeditions Pass - $2,000

All features of the exploration pass included, with the ability to access more sections of the park.

Executive Pass - $10,000

The highest tier with helicopter and lab tour, a week-long stay at the park, front-row seating at events, and more.
