About The Exploration Pass - $250

Jurassic Park's exploration pass grants you access to common areas of the park, with the abilities to purchase tours and expeditions as add-ons. You will be given access to the park's key zones, with this pass being ideal for those who want an introduction to what Jurassic Park has to offer.

There is a $300 insurance and liability fee for any injuries that may occur when interacting with prehistoric wildlife. Extra taxes apply for all tier levels.
