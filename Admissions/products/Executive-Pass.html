Jurassic Park Admissions

About The Executive Pass - $500

Jurassic Park's Executive Pass offers you an adventure of a life time! You get an exclusive helicopter tour during your stay, with aerial views of the park. You get to experience behind-the-scenes lab tours, meeting people who brought the park to life such as John Hammond, Dr. Henry Wu, Ian Malcolm, and VIP treatment to all park events!

There is a $300 insurance and liability fee for any injuries that may occur when interacting with prehistoric wildlife. Extra taxes apply for all tier levels.
