Jurassic Park Admissions

About The Seasonal Expedition Pass - $2,200

Jurassic Park's expedition pass grants you access to more areas of the park than what the exploration pass offered, with the ability to purchase tours and expeditions as add-ons at a reduced price. You will be given access to more exclusive park zones, with this pass being ideal for those who want a more in-depth guide to what Jurassic Park has to offer for a whole season.

There is a $300 insurance and liability fee for any injuries that may occur when interacting with prehistoric wildlife. Extra taxes apply for all tier levels.
