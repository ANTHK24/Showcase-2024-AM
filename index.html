Basic Passes

Exploration Pass - $1,000

The exploration pass lets you enter the basic park zones with a guided tour.

Expeditions Pass - $2,000

All features of the exploration pass included, with the ability to access more sections of the park.

More Information

Executive Pass - $10,000

The highest tier with helicopter and lab tour, a week-long stay at the park, front-row seating at events, and more.

Seasonal Passes

Seasonal Explorer - $15,000

Features of the exploration pass included with yearly access and a 5% discount on park services.

Seasonal Expedition Pass - $30,000

Features of the expedition pass included with yearly access and a 10% discount on park services.

Seasonal Executive - $96,000

The highest tier of the seasonal passes which includes all the benefits of the executive pass free access to all park services.

There is a $300 insurance and liability fee for any injuries that may occur when interacting with prehistoric wildlife. Extra taxes apply for all tier levels.
